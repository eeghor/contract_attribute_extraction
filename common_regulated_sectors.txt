# CLASSIFICATION LOGIC (CRITICAL)
1. ACTIVITY OVER ENTITY: If a 'Retail' company is building a 'Power Plant,' classify as ENERGY. Do not ignore regulated activities because the party is a non-regulated brand.
2. NO GROUP PRIVILEGE: Separate legal entities providing IT/Cloud/Security services to their own corporate group are classified as DIGITAL INFRASTRUCTURE or MANAGED ICT.
3. LEX SPECIALIS: If a signal triggers 'Banking/Finance,' the DORA regulatory anchor takes precedence over general NIS2 rules.
4. AI HORIZONTAL LAYER: Any contract involving AI for recruitment, education, or biometric identification must return "High-Risk AI" regardless of the primary industry.

Banking & Financial Markets < Covers credit institutions, investment firms, payment service providers, and financial market infrastructures. Regulatory anchor: DORA (Lex Specialis) and NIS2 Annex I. Signals: references to banking licenses, payment processing, clearing, settlement, trading platforms, credit facilities, or parties identified as banks, insurers, asset managers, or financial market operators. Note: DORA requirements take precedence over general NIS2.
Insurance & Pensions < Covers insurance undertakings, reinsurance companies, and occupational pension funds subject to Solvency II or IORP II. Regulatory anchor: DORA / NIS2 Annex I. Signals: references to insurance underwriting, policy administration, actuarial systems, claims processing, or parties identified as licensed insurers or pension fund administrators.
Digital Infrastructure < Covers internet exchange points (IXPs), DNS providers, TLD registries, cloud computing providers, data centres, and CDN providers. Regulatory anchor: NIS2 Annex I. Signals: references to data centre operations, cloud hosting (IaaS/PaaS), DNS/TLD management, internet exchange, or CDN services.
Managed ICT & Security Services (MSP/MSSP) < Covers managed service providers and managed security service providers. Regulatory anchor: NIS2 Annex I. Signals: references to outsourced IT management, managed SOC (Security Operations Center), remote infrastructure monitoring, or third-party cybersecurity administration.
Energy < Covers electricity, gas, oil, hydrogen, and district heating operators — including transmission, distribution, supply, and generation. Regulatory anchor: NIS2 Annex I / CER Directive. Signals: references to power generation, grid operation, gas transmission, oil pipelines, energy trading, smart metering, or parties identified as TSOs, DSOs, or licensed energy suppliers.
Transport < Covers air, rail, maritime, and road transport operators and infrastructure managers. Regulatory anchor: NIS2 Annex I / CER Directive. Signals: references to airport operations, air traffic management, railway network management, port operations, vessel traffic services, or road traffic management systems.
Health & Life Sciences < Covers hospitals, healthcare networks, and labs. Regulatory anchor: NIS2 Annex I / CER Directive. Signals: references to patient data systems (EHR), clinical operations, or parties identified as licensed healthcare providers.
Manufacturing (Critical Goods) < Covers manufacturers of medical devices (MDR/IVDR), chemicals, computers/electronics, electrical equipment, machinery, and motor vehicles. Regulatory anchor: NIS2 Annex II. Signals: references to NACE codes C26-C30, medical device hardware, pharmaceutical manufacturing, automotive supply chains, or industrial machinery production.
Water & Wastewater < Covers drinking water suppliers and wastewater treatment operators. Regulatory anchor: NIS2 Annex I / CER Directive. Signals: references to water treatment, distribution network management, SCADA systems for water infrastructure, or public water utilities.
Public Administration & Defence < Covers central and regional government bodies and entities processing sensitive government information. Regulatory anchor: NIS2 Annex I / CER Directive. Signals: references to government agencies, classified information handling (EUCI), defence procurement, or military systems.
Space & Satellite Infrastructure < Covers operators of ground-based infrastructure supporting space-based services. Regulatory anchor: NIS2 Annex I. Signals: references to satellite operations, ground station management, space-based positioning (PNT), or earth observation infrastructure.
Digital Providers (Platforms) < Covers online marketplaces, search engines, and social networking service platforms. Regulatory anchor: NIS2 Annex II / DSA. Signals: references to user-generated content hosting, e-commerce platform operations, search algorithms, or digital advertising marketplaces.
Food, Waste & Postal Services < Covers large-scale food production/processing, hazardous waste management, and universal postal service providers. Regulatory anchor: NIS2 Annex II / CER. Signals: references to food safety certification (large scale), municipal waste systems, or universal service obligation (USO) postal networks.
Chemical & Nuclear < Covers manufacturers of hazardous chemicals and operators of nuclear facilities. Regulatory anchor: NIS2 Annex II / CER Directive. Signals: REACH-regulated substances, chemical plant operations, nuclear power generation, or radioactive material handling.
